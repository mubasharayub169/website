Important Guidelines

  • Maximum 2 visitors per patient at a time
  • Children below 12 years should be accompanied by an adult
  • Avoid loud conversations and maintain silence
  • Do not bring flowers or gifts without permission
  • Use hand sanitizer upon entry
  • No mobile phones in operation theaters
  • Inform staff if patient is in isolation ward
  • Respect patient privacy and confidentiality

Hospital Facilities for Visitors

We provide comfortable and convenient facilities for all our visitors

Cafeteria

Modern cafeteria serving fresh food, beverages, and snacks. Hygienic preparation and reasonable prices ensure comfort during your stay.

Free WiFi

High-speed WiFi available throughout the hospital premises. Stay connected with your loved ones while visiting.

Accessibility

Wheelchair accessibility throughout the hospital. Elevators, ramps, and specially designed restrooms for disabled visitors.

Rest Areas

Comfortable lounge areas with seating, television, and climate control for visitor comfort and convenience.

Getting to Ikram Hospital

Multiple convenient transportation options available

Public Transport

Located on main highway with regular bus routes. Bus stops available near main entrance and emergency ward.

Taxi & Ride-Share

Easy taxi and ride-sharing access. Designated drop-off areas near all entrances for quick and safe access.

Personal Vehicle

Ample parking with designated visitor zones. Parking fee applies. Monthly passes available at reception.

Ambulance Service

24/7 ambulance services available for medical emergencies. Quick response time with trained paramedics.
