Our Diagnostic Services

X-Ray Services

High-resolution digital X-ray imaging for chest, bones, and soft tissues. Fast, safe, and highly accurate.

  • Chest X-rays
  • Bone imaging
  • Digital radiography
  • Minimal radiation

CT Scan (CAT Scan)

Multi-slice CT technology providing detailed cross-sectional images of the body for complex diagnostic needs.

  • Brain imaging
  • Chest & abdomen
  • Soft tissue analysis
  • 3D reconstruction

MRI Scan

Advanced magnetic resonance imaging without radiation. Perfect for detailed soft tissue and neurological imaging.

  • Brain & spinal cord
  • Joint imaging
  • Soft tissue analysis
  • No radiation exposure

Echocardiography

Ultrasound imaging of the heart to assess cardiac function, valve performance, and structural abnormalities.

  • 2D echocardiography
  • Doppler ultrasound
  • Stress testing
  • Real-time imaging

Ultrasound

Safe and non-invasive imaging using sound waves to examine organs, tissues, and blood flow.

  • Abdominal imaging
  • Obstetric ultrasound
  • Vascular imaging
  • Real-time guidance

Laboratory Services

Comprehensive blood tests, pathology, and microbiology services with rapid turnaround times.

  • Complete blood count
  • Chemistry panels
  • Microbiology testing
  • Rapid results
