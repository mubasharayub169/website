Coronary Care Unit (CCU)

Our Coronary Care Unit provides specialized care for patients with acute cardiac conditions, offering advanced monitoring and treatment for heart attacks, arrhythmias, and cardiac emergencies.

  • 24/7 cardiac monitoring and telemetry
  • Advanced life support and resuscitation
  • Interventional cardiology services
  • Post-operative cardiac care
