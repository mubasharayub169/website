Support and Quality

Complaints and Feedback

Your experience matters. We review every complaint seriously to improve patient care and service quality.

Please provide clear details so our quality team can investigate and respond effectively.

How to Submit

Use our Contact Page and mention "Complaint" in the subject or message details.

Information to Include

Include your name, contact number, visit date, and a clear description of the concern.

Review Process

Each complaint is reviewed by relevant department staff and escalated when required.

Response Timeline

We aim to acknowledge complaints within 2 business days and provide updates during resolution.

For urgent medical situations, contact emergency services immediately instead of using complaint channels.
